Course Material

This project synthesizes material from the following lectures in ASTR/PHSC 12720, Spring 2026, taught at The University of Chicago:

  • Lecture 2 — Solar System Overview (star comparison, system architecture)
  • Lectures 6 & 7 — Radial Velocities (discovery method, RV equation, m sin i)
  • Lecture 10 — Direct Imaging (angular resolution, coronagraphy, future prospects)
  • Lecture 14 — Planet Formation (nebular hypothesis, core accretion, migration)
  • Lecture 16 — Planet Atmospheres (atmospheric escape mechanisms, XUV erosion)
  • Lecture 17 — Habitability and Biosignatures (habitable zone, tidal locking, biosignatures)
